About Me

Namaste! 🙏

I’m Shruti Kelkar - an artist 🎨, dancer 💃, and storyteller at heart 💗.

My journey into the world of art began over 15 years ago 📖, with a humble sketchpad in one hand ✍️ and ghungroos around my ankles 👣. What started as a childhood passion has now become my life’s soul-language 🪔, whether I’m painting vibrant Mandala patterns ☀️, creating soulful wall art 🖼️, or gracefully expressing through Kathak, the classical dance form I’ve been dedicated to for the past 18 years ✨.

I’m a Kathak Visharad and currently sharing this joy by teaching others 👩‍🏫. Just like art, dance too is a form of prayer for me 🌸🙏.

For me, art is not just decoration, it’s an emotion 🌟. It can heal 💫, uplift 🌈, and connect souls without saying a word 🤍. Through my work, I try to bring a little more warmth, color, and calm into the spaces we live in 🏡. Thank you for visiting my world 🌍. I hope my creations bring the same joy to your heart as they do to mine 💕. Let’s celebrate the beauty of our roots 🌿, one painting 🎨, one performance 💃, one soulful moment at a time ✨.
